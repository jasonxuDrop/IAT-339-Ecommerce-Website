Born of a minimalist but also nostalgic desire, the boop65, elegant and voluptuous case is heavly inspired by 80's micro-computers such as the Sinclair ZX Spectrum, but with a 7 degree typing angle in order to have a better typing experience.

This slick design is a perfect match for those in need of a classy keyboard without any sortes of flourishes that can be eye-catching especially when paired with : e-white, gray or black, but it can also fade into your setup with a more standard colorway such as : Rose gold, purple, blue or polycarbonate.

That keyboard would please any keyboard aficionados but also people who wanna jump into the rabbit hole since 2 versions will be available: solderable & hot-swap.

- KBDfans x Biip

KEYBOARD SPECIFICATIONS

  • top case and bottom case
  • brass weight
  • plate ( Material: Aluminum E-White/ Brass/ FR4/ Polycarbonate Version: soldered/ hot-swap )
  • PCB ( soldered version-RGB underglow, hot-swap version-RGB on the top; Both support VIA configurator; Type-C interface )
  • Cherry screw-in stabilizers 60 set
  • Gaskets/ Screws/ Rubber feet
